What is A.I. Motion Pictures?

A new system/tool for picture creation based on machine learning (A.I.). A database of creative input is created and the machine is “taught” to create visual graphics to purpose.

Why does A.I. Motion Pictures look so different?

Humans think linear and identify objects separate from their environment. However, due to its computational capacity, A.I. has a more quantum creation approach to graphics and it does not separate the subject from its surroundings. Hence, we can create images that go beyond “rational” human experience and enable us visualize emotional realities that had no visuals translations to date.
